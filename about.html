A magna cum laude graduate of CU Boulder’s Environmental Design program, PJ is available for new opportunities to provide his determined and professional approach to product design. He believes that communication and pragmatism are imperative to the success of any project, and is sure to impress with his diligence, amicable personality and impressive skill set.

In addition to his work as a designer, PJ is also an accomplished musician and educator. He has performed and taught across a wide variety of styles as both a percussionist and guitarist. When he’s not at the drawing board, you are sure to catch him playing local venues and offering lessons.

PJ is currently based out of New York City in Brooklyn.
