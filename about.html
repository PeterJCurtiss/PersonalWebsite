A magna cum laude graduate of CU Boulder’s Environmental Design program, PJ Curtiss is currently available for new opportunities to provide his determined and professional approach to creative design and product visualization. His impressive skill set, amicable personality and strong work ethic make him an ideal candidate for any job.

Originally a native of Boulder, Colorado, PJ will be based out of New York City beginning July 2022.
